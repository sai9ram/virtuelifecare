Our Story

Virtue Life Care based at Bangalore, Karnataka, India,
Committed to improving patients' lives and serving the healthcare community through innovation

Virtue Life Care enhances the quality of life through research and development, manufacturing and marketing of pharmaceutical and health care products. Our belief is driven by strong competitive health advantage in the domestic market which lies in chronic therapeutic areas including Gynaecology, Pediatrics, Gastroenterology, Cardiology, Diabetology, Endocrinology, Neurology, Nephrology, Oncology, dermatology, Dentistry, Vitamins, Minerals and Nutritional products.

Virtue Life Care committed to innovation to find new ways to provide affordable medicines with total commitment towards quality.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to Develop Innovative New Therapeutics to Improve Human Health and be a Front Runner in Research and Development of New Therapeutics.

Our Vision

We Endeavor to make Virtue Life Care as a major pharmaceutical company offering novel therapeutics to treat significant medical needs, with total commitment to quality.
